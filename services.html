Our Services

Palayan provides comprehensive support to local farmers and agri-entrepreneurs. Our services include:

  • Expert agricultural consultation
  • Access to farming equipment and tools
  • Certified seeds and sustainable fertilizers
  • Soil health analysis and irrigation planning
  • Farming seminars and training programs
  • Harvest and post-harvest marketing assistance
